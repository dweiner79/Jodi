What is Color Analysis?

Color Has the Power to Transform How the World Sees You

Color analysis is the art and science of identifying the colors that naturally harmonize with your unique skin tone, eye color, and hair — the colors that make you look awake, vibrant, and effortlessly beautiful.

When you wear your best colors, your skin glows, your eyes brighten, and fine lines fade into the background. When you wear the wrong ones, even expensive clothes can leave you looking tired or washed out.

Life in Color Studios uses the proven seasonal color system to reveal your personal palette — so you can get dressed with confidence every single day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Come with a bare, clean face (no makeup if possible) and wear a plain white or grey top. This gives us the most accurate reading of your natural coloring. We'll drape fabric swatches around your face, so your clothing won't affect the results.

In-person one-on-one sessions typically run about 90 minutes. Color parties vary depending on group size, usually 2.5–4 hours for a group of 4–6 guests.

You'll leave with a personalized color palette card featuring your best colors, along with guidance on clothing, makeup, accessories, and hair colors that complement your season.

Currently, Life in Color Studios focuses on in-person sessions in the Metro Atlanta area to provide the most accurate results. Reach out and we can chat about your situation!

The traditional system includes 4 seasons (Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter), each with sub-seasons for a more nuanced result. During your session, we'll identify your specific season and sub-type for a truly personalized palette.

Life in Color Studios is based in Metro Atlanta, GA. I'm available throughout the greater Atlanta area for both private sessions and color parties. Contact me to discuss your location.
